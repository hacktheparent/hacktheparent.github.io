Hack the Parent

Parenting is a war of attrition –– your spouse is your comrade, in the trenches with you.

However,The enemy is not the child –– the enemy is you.

Well, sometimes the child is the enemy. Sometimes it's the school or the grandparents or the various sytems you live in.

You can't control those things though –– you have the most control over you.
